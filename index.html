My Favourite Artists

Bon Iver

Bon Iver is, without a doubt, the best musical act I have ever seen perform live. Legit cried along with my brother and not even remotely embarassed. Absolutely beautiful and love the variety you find in every album they put out. Best song? Holocene.

Chance The Rapper

As evidenced by my Spotify.me profile (check yours here), Chance holds the definite honour of singing my most listened to track "All We Got". Coloring Book is my favourite hip hop album ever.

Mumford & Sons

Claim to fame time: Two of them went to my school, and I had the same piano teacher as the pianist. Seen them live four times now, one of my all time favourite artists, and I cannot wait for their new album to come out. Listen to their new single Guiding Light.
